About us

Welcome to T-Equality! This Website is a non-profit project to support children in getting their first knowledge in IT. The Tech-Industry is one of the most rapidly growing ones in the world and of course also one of the best paid ones. In a men's world we are proud to introduce this project that is not made by any men nor is focused on men. What we are trying to achieve is letting young children, regarless of their gender, learn important subjects for their future career and show them that it can be so much fun.

For who this website is made

Target of our website are children of all gender between 8 and 16 years that are interested in IT or children that never had the access to learning IT-Stuff. This is an easy way to introduce and inspire young folks for the tech-field.
